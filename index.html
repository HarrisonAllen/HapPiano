Project Name: Piano With Adaptive Tactile Feedback

Team Members: Michael Shumikhin, Harrison Allen

Our idea

It can be challenging to learn how to play the piano. Our tactile piano gradually reduces support over time. The idea is to detect how well the player is playing and then adjust the frequency of tactile guidance accordingly.

How it works

  • 16 keys -> Laser cut (Fast iteration)
  • Key mechanism -> Dowel rod + spring (easy & effective)
  • Detect key press -> Switch (Small & fast response)
  • Tactile nubs -> Laser cut (Specific shape)
  • Actuate tactile nubs -> Servos (Fine control)
  • Control -> RaspberryPi3 w/ GPIO extension (Lots of docs, Python üòÅ, and more easily enables dynamic programs)
  • Audio -> Speaker connected to RaspberryPi through AUX
  • Adaption -> Software controls tactile assistance based on user performance
